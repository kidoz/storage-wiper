standards and methods:

- **DoD 5220.22-M**: U.S. Department of Defense standard (now superseded)
- **Peter Gutmann Method**: As described in "Secure Deletion of Data from
  Magnetic and Solid-State Memory" (1996)
- **Bruce Schneier Method**: As described in "Applied Cryptography" (1996)
- **VSITR**: German BSI standard
- **GOST R 50739-95**: Russian Federation standard
- **ATA Secure Erase**: ANSI ATA standard

## Security Considerations

This software is provided for **defensive security purposes only**. It is
intended for:

- Secure decommissioning of storage devices
- Preparation of devices for disposal or resale
- Compliance with data sanitization requirements
- Legitimate data privacy protection

This software is NOT intended for:

- Destroying evidence of illegal activities
- Unauthorized access to or destruction of others' data
- Circumventing data recovery for malicious purposes
- Any illegal or unethical purposes

Users must comply with all applicable laws, including but not limited to:
- Data protection and privacy regulations (GDPR, CCPA, etc.)
- Computer fraud and abuse laws
- Evidence preservation requirements
- Organizational policies and procedures
